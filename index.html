Boring History

  • Born at Netscape around 1995 as "LiveScript".
  • Quickly renamed to JavaScript.
  • Support added to IE in v3.0 in 1996.
  • Standardized as ECMAScript in 1997.
  • Now on ECMAScript v5.1, with v6.0 in progress.
